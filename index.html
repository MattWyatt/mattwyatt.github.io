my work

inhabitants of the archives

Here, recorded in the space of these archives, are some of the projects I've worked on. This digital space was created in hopes of giving life to these projects; that's why they all have names. All software can be defined as an interface: human to machine. That interface, however, being developed by humans, opens itself to inheriting little bits and pieces of our humanity and our names. A program with a recognizable name feels much nicer than a program with a cold and functional name. When using software, one must feel as if they are talking to a computer, not using it. This is my philosophy on software design.
